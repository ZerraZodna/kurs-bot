# Privacy Notice (GDPR)

**Last updated:** 2024-10-01

This notice explains how Kurs Bot collects, uses, and protects personal data in compliance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## 1) Controller
- **Controller:** Kurs Bot
- **Contact:** support@example.com
- **DPO:** dpo@example.com (if applicable)

## 2) Data We Collect
- User identifiers (channel, external ID)
- Contact details (email, phone number)
- Profile information (name, preferences)
- Conversation content and derived memories
- Usage metadata (timestamps, message status)

## 3) Purposes & Legal Basis
- Provide coaching and lesson delivery (Art. 6(1)(b) contract; Art. 6(1)(a) consent where required)
- Improve service quality and reliability (Art. 6(1)(f) legitimate interests)
- Compliance, security, and audit logging (Art. 6(1)(c) legal obligation)

## 4) Retention
- Conversation logs: 30 days unless required for support or compliance
- Memories: retained while active; archived items purged after 365 days
- GDPR request logs and audit logs: retained for compliance as required

## 5) Sharing & Transfers
- Data may be processed by sub-processors for hosting and messaging delivery
- International transfers use appropriate safeguards (e.g., SCCs)

## 6) Your Rights
- Access, rectification, erasure
- Restriction of processing, data portability, objection
- Withdraw consent at any time
- Lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority

## 7) Automated Decision-Making
- Automated processing is used to personalize content. No legal or similarly significant decisions are made solely by automated means.

## 8) Contact
To exercise your rights, email: privacy@example.com
