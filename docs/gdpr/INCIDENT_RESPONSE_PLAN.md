# Incident & Breach Response Plan

**Last updated:** 2024-10-01

## Scope
Applies to all personal data processing systems and integrations.

## Roles & Contacts
- Incident Manager:
- Security Lead:
- DPO (if applicable):
- Legal:

## Severity Levels
- Low / Medium / High / Critical

## Workflow
1. Detect and triage incident
2. Contain and mitigate
3. Assess impact and affected data
4. Notify authority within 72 hours if required
5. Notify data subjects if high risk
6. Post-incident review and remediation

## Evidence & Logging
- Preserve logs and audit trails
- Record timeline and actions
